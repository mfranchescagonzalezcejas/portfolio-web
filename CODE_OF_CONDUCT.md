# Code of Conduct

This is a small personal portfolio project. Contributions and discussions should stay respectful, focused, and safe for collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and professional.
- Assume good intent, but correct issues clearly.
- Keep feedback focused on the repository, documentation, code, or workflow.
- Respect privacy and do not share personal, client, or confidential information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive comments.
- Sharing secrets, exploit details, or sensitive reports in public issues.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block users when behavior harms the project or participants.

For sensitive security concerns, follow `SECURITY.md` instead of opening a public issue.
